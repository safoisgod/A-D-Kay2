Politics for Sale

By: Ampem-Daako Kay

Published on: December 4, 2023

★★★★☆ 3.9/5 (26 Reviews)

"Politics for Sale" is a gripping political fiction that explores the dark side of politics, power, and corruption. In a world where integrity is often compromised, the story delves into the moral dilemmas faced by political leaders, as well as the choices that shape nations. The novel serves as a warning about the consequences of a corrupt system while questioning the price of political power.

About the Author

Ampem-Daako Kay is a renowned author, educator, and cultural advocate. His works explore African heritage, history, and identity, offering insightful commentary on themes like wisdom, leadership, and the impact of colonialism.

Through books like Culture Brewed in an African Pot, Politics for Sale, and French Composition, he brings together education, culture, and social discourse to inspire and inform a wide range of readers.
